Thapar Movie Club

INTRODUCTION Thapar Movie Club (TMC) encourages TIET fraternity in all aspects of movie making. Over past couple of years TMC has promoted a number of projects that includes full movie, short movies and web series in association with the club. MEMBERSHIP All students, staff and faculty members are de novo members of Thapar Movie Club and are welcome to explore their imaginative talent before and behind the camera related to movie making. ACTIVITIES - (1) MOVIE MAKING: TMC provides logistic, administrative or any other support for movie making to all the members of the club on individual or team basis. (2) COMPETITIONS TMC organises movie making competitions from time to time. Announcements are made at the TMCâ€™s official website. (3) WORKSHOPS & SEMINARS related to movie making are organised from time to time. Eminent personalities are invited for an interaction with the club members. OTHER ACTIVITIES TMC welcomes any activity that members may be interested in getting initiated. Please get in touch with us at tmc@thapar.edu with a written proposal detailing the activity details its schedule and the kind of support required from the club.
