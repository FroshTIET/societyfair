IAESTE CI-TIET

The International Association for the Exchange of Students for Technical Experience (IAESTE) is a non-political, non-profit, international organization that serves as a platform for exchanging students for technical work experience abroad. The Association was founded in January 1948 at the Imperial College, London (which, today, is the Worldâ€™s eighth-best university according to the QS Rankings 2021). Starting with just 10 members, the geographical coverage soon spread to most of Europe and beyond. Today, it has more than 85 member countries and India is one of them. The main aim of the IAESTE is to provide students with purely technical and highly professional internships (with a stipend) ranging from 4 to 52 weeks.
