IETE Students' Forum

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) Students' Forum is India's leading recognized, professional and national society devoted to the advancement of Science and Technology. It is is a National Society and also the official electronics club of the Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala. The main objective of this society is to organize the technical events that involve electronics, telecommunication, and IT as a core area. -> It conducts various technical events and infuses in the students the true essence of Electronics by imparting practical knowledge through various workshops, technical talks, and minor projects. -> It organizes various workshops for the students to teach them topics like Arduino from scratch, Line Follower Bot, Bluetooth controlled Robots, Robo Soccer Bots, and many more.. -> It helps students to acquire the necessary skills required and achieve much more at an unmatched scale, with both software and interactive learning. -> It organizes the college's most exciting and the biggest annual technical fest called ELEMENTOS. Till now ELEMENTOS has witnessed its 5 versions. In February 2020, ELEMENTOS 5.0 was conducted which was a huge success in all respects.
