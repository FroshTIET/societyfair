IEI Thapar Students' Chapter

IEl aims to mentor young Engineering minds who have a passion for Engineering and wish to hone their technical skills. The Institution of Engineers India [IEI] is a statutory body to promote and advance the engineering and technology, established in 1920 and incorporated by Royal Charter in 1935. It is the largest multi-disciplinary professional body of engineers encompassing 15 engineering disciplines with a membership of more than 820 thousand and serving the nation for more than 9 decades. The IEI has its headquarters located in Kolkata with national presence through more than hundred Centres and several Overseas Chapters, Foras and Organ IEI (Institution of Engineers India) Thapar Chapter is a technical society of Thapar University which organizes various workshops, technical exams, lecture series by industry experts. This society is open for all technical branches of engineering.
