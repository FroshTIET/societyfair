Creative Computing Society

Creative Computing Society- the oldest and the best tech society of TIET, aims to bring all the tech geeks of the institute together so that they all can learn and rise. For 16 years, CCS has provided the students with an amazing environment that has yielded the following results.
1. We host technical events on the largest scale in college. For 15 years celebration, we had the CEO of GeeksforGeeks.
2. For last 3+ consecutive years, highest package of the college was bagged by the members of CCS.
3. Many major projects of college are developed and maintained by the members of CCS. Consequently, CCS members are preferred by the college for the projects. 4. CCS is the only tech society that organises 2 techfests every year.
5. Hackathons organised by CCS are sponsored by amazing tech giants like GitHub, Devfolio, Matic and flooded with pizzas and Red Bulls.
